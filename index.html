Pine Bluff, Arkansas

The town I was born in. It is the tenth largest city in the state of Arkansas(population of 40,779). The city is situated in the Southeast section of the Arkansas Delta and straddles the Arkansas Timberlands region to the west.

Conway, Arkansas

This is the city I moved to in high school and the same city I graduated high school from. Conway is the 7th largest city in Arkansas. Conway has experienced growth from big companies such as Acxiom, and Insight Enterprises.
